Gullas College of Medicine is a Vibrant Multi-Cultural Campus with members of all cultures and nationalities, striving together for excellence.

What Makes A Gullas Doctor?

A Gullas Doctor is someone for whom the pursuit of excellence, both professional and personal — is a way of life. If this describes you, we encourage you to apply to Gullas College of Medicine to education, research, clinical care and service.

Our History

University of Visayas has a proud legacy, being the first university in the region. The Gullas College of Medicine became part of it’s legacy in 1977 and was founded with the sole mission to produce intellectuals that excel in clinical practice, medical research and most of all, compassion.

Research & Leadership

As part of its mission to alleviate human suffering from disease, UVGCM encourages and supports innovation and research by its talented community of students and faculty. Our students have bagged various accolades and has been identified as community leaders.
